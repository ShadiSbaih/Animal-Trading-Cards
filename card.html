Tiger

A layer of fur on the tiger's skin helps it stay warm in cold environments.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: Up to 3.3 meters
  • Average Lifespan:10-15 Years
  • Habitat: Rainforests and temperate forestss

The tiger, with its striking orange coat marked with black stripes, is among the most recognizable of all wild animals. They can grow up to 3.3 meters in length, making them the largest members of the cat family. Tigers are solitary predators, primarily found in varied habitats from dense forests to grasslands where they prey on animals such as deer and wild boar.
